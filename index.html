Welcome

Established in 2013 Northern Lights 4 branches are some of the most visited restaurants in Camiguin. Famous for their Pizza and all Natural Homemade Ice Cream there is something on the menu for the whole family to enjoy at affordable prices.

Discover More

Home Made Ice Cream

Our Ice Cream is made from locally farmed Milk, in our dedicated Air Conditioned Ice Cream Kitchen. We only use fresh fruit for our flavours so availability will vary with the seasons.
